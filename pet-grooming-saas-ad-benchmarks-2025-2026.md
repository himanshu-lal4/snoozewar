## 11. BEST CHANNELS FOR VERTICAL SaaS CUSTOMER ACQUISITION

**Ranked by effectiveness for pet grooming SaaS:**

### Tier 1 — Highest ROI
1. **Referrals / Word of Mouth** — CAC: $141–$200. Pet groomers are a tight community (Facebook groups, trade shows, grooming schools). Referral programs are the #1 channel.
2. **SEO / Organic Content** — CAC: $205–$290 long-term. Target long-tail keywords like "best pet grooming software", "how to manage grooming appointments". Low competition niche.
3. **Google Search Ads (high-intent)** — CAC: $200–$500 est. Target "pet grooming software" and competitor keywords. Low volume but high intent.

### Tier 2 — Good Supporting Channels
4. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** — Target pet grooming business owners in FB groups, lookalike audiences from existing customers. CPC: $1–$4 for B2B targeting.
5. **Partnerships** — Pet supply distributors, grooming schools, pet industry associations. Ecosystem-led growth is rising.
6. **Product-Led Growth (PLG)** — Free tier or free trial with quick time-to-value. Freemium converts at 2.6%, opt-in trial at 18.2%.

### Tier 3 — Lower Priority for This Niche
7. **YouTube** — Grooming tutorial content with software placement
8. **Trade shows / Industry events** — High cost but relationship-building
9. **LinkedIn Ads** — Poor fit for pet grooming audience
10. **App Store optimization** — If mobile-first, but web acquisition is primary

### Key Strategy Insight
- Existing customers generate **40% of new ARR** across B2B SaaS (50%+ above $50M ARR)
- Partnerships predicted to become primary growth engine by 2026 (Forrester)
- AI-powered outbound is emerging but overkill for this niche
