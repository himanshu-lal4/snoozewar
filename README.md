# SnoozeWar — Research Repository

**snoozewar.com** | @snoozewar

## What This Is
Complete research and strategy foundation for **SnoozeWar** — a mobile app that helps people stick to their required schedule by understanding their biology and enforcing the habits that make it work. Built on chronotype science, not motivation.

**Positioning:** "69% of people wake up in the wrong timezone. It's called social jet lag. We fix it."
