## Top 10 Insights Across All Research

1. **The full-cycle thesis is validated by data** — later bedtime directly correlates with more snoozing. Fix the night, fix the morning.
2. **ADHD is not a niche** — 366M adults, 80% have sleep disorders, 73-78% delayed circadian rhythms. Product must account for this.
3. **They want enforcement, not encouragement** — "I really hate Alarmy, but that's a good thing."
4. **Chronotype awareness is near-zero** — the onboarding quiz is the most powerful conversion moment. Reframes years of self-blame in 3 minutes.
5. **They search night and morning separately** — nobody connects them. Our marketing angle: "These aren't two problems. They're one loop."
6. **#socialjetlag is unclaimed on TikTok** — biggest first-mover organic opportunity.
7. **82% of trial starts AND 55% of cancellations happen on Day 0** — onboarding is everything.
8. **January cohorts are the worst quality** (D30: 3-10%) — don't overspend on paid acquisition in January.
9. **What they actually want: control (40%) + freedom from guilt (25%)** — not productivity, not health.
10. **The "Be Mean to Me" AI trend validates the Dare Engine** — users are actively seeking tough-love digital personalities.
