## Strategic Summary

Social jet lag is an obscure but scientifically validated concept (Roenneberg 2006, 26+ PubMed papers) that affects 69-70% of the population. No app, no creator, no brand owns this term yet. We claim it — becoming the RISE of social jet lag (RISE turned "sleep debt" into a $650K/month app).

---

## The Positioning

**Primary:** "69% of people wake up in the wrong timezone. It's called social jet lag."

**Secondary:** "Social jet lag doubles your diabetes risk, kills your mornings, and no alarm app even knows it exists."

**Solution:** "We're the first app that detects your chronotype, shifts your clock, and keeps it there."

**CTA:** "Find your social jet lag score. 90 seconds. Free."

## Three-Layer Strategy

### Layer 1: Own the Term (SEO/ASO)
- Rank for "social jet lag app" (zero competition today)
- Rank for "chronotype quiz", "why can't I wake up", "what is my chronotype"
- Free web tool: Social Jet Lag Calculator (4 questions → score → email capture → app funnel)
- App Store description leads with social jet lag framing

### Layer 2: Content Engine (TikTok + Reels)
- Pre-launch content calendar: 12 weeks of social jet lag education
- Hook formula: shocking stat → identity recognition → solution
- Format: short-form video, visually demonstrate dual clocks (body clock vs alarm clock)
- Ride existing trends: #sleepmaxxing (100M+ views), #morningroutine (8B+ monthly)
- Create new trend: #socialjetlag, #wrongtimezone

### Layer 3: Fear Hook → Aspiration Resolve
- Hook with consequence: "doubles diabetes risk", "fighting your DNA"
- Resolve with empowerment: "find your perfect wake time", "shift your clock in 14 days"
- Based on: fear appeals meta-analysis (APA) — gain-framed works better for prevention, but fear grabs attention

2. "What time do you wake up on work days?" → time picker

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk:** Social jet lag stays too niche — people don't search for it.
**Mitigation:** We create the demand through content. RISE didn't wait for "sleep debt" searches — they made people care. Our TikTok content, web tool, and App Store presence build the category.

**Risk:** Fear-based health messaging backfires (feels manipulative).
**Mitigation:** Always pair fear hook with de-shaming + empowerment. "It's not your fault (it's biology) + here's how to fix it (the protocol)." Never guilt. Always science.

**Risk:** "Social jet lag" sounds medical — people think they need a doctor, not an app.
**Mitigation:** Frame as wellness optimization, not medical treatment. "Like a fitness tracker for your body clock." Include safety screens for when a doctor IS needed.

Nobody else is doing this because:
