## Naming DNA (User's Taste)
- Invented compound words, 2-3 syllables
- Real concept buried inside (chronos, circadian, behavior, habit)
- Hard consonants: K, R, D, X
- No cute spelling tricks
- Must feel like a real word in some language
- Must work globally: US, India, Japan, UK, Germany
