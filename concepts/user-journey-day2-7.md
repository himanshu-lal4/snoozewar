# User Journey: Day 2-7 (Trial Period)

*Created: April 2026*
*Purpose: What keeps users coming back during the 7-day trial, and why they convert on Day 7.*

---

## The Trial's ONE Job

**Make the cost of leaving feel higher than the cost of staying.**

Key data:
- 55% of trial cancellations happen on Day 0 — if they made it to Day 2, they're in the better half
- 7-day streak = 3.6x retention (Duolingo)
- Gamification novelty dip starts Week 2 — Week 1 is still honeymoon
- Variable rewards beat fixed (Schultz 1997) — same notification every morning kills engagement

## The 5 Mechanics Running During Days 2-7

### 1. The Score Ladder

They see their score changing daily. Good nights → higher mornings. Bad nights → app shows exactly why.

```
Day 1: 64  ██████░░░░
Day 2: 67  ██████▓░░░
Day 3: 72  ███████░░░
Day 4: 68  ██████▓░░░  (went to bed late)
Day 5: 74  ███████▓░░
Day 6: 71  ███████░░░
Day 7: 76  ████████░░
```

The dip on Day 4 makes the pattern real — it wasn't a fluke. By Day 5, they have enough data to see their own pattern.

### 2. The Streak Counter

Every day it grows. Every day the cost of breaking increases. Losses felt 2-2.5x more than gains (Kahneman). By Day 7, breaking the streak = losing a week, not just one morning.

### 3. The Dare Engine Variety

Never the same line twice in Week 1. Minimum 50 unique lines across all touchpoints.

| Day | Morning Dare Example (Balanced tone) |
|-----|--------------------------------------|
| 2 | "Day 2. The real test. Yesterday could've been a fluke. Prove it wasn't." |
| 3 | "3 days. Most people quit here. You're not most people. Or are you?" |
| 4 | "Day 4. The honeymoon's over. Now it's discipline. Let's go." |
| 5 | "Day 5. You went to bed late last night. We noticed. Your score noticed. Get up anyway." |
| 6 | "Day 6. Tomorrow your trial ends. Your streak doesn't have to." |
| 7 | "Day 7. One week. You did something 82% of people never do. Don't let it be a fluke." |

### 4. Progressive Revelation

New things appear as they earn them — the app gets smarter each day because it has more data:

| Day | What Unlocks |
|-----|-------------|
| Day 1 | Core: alarm, mission, score, Night Mode |
| Day 2 | Score comparison (yesterday vs today) |
| Day 3 | First streak milestone celebration |
| Day 4 | Night pattern insight (3-night data) |
| Day 5 | Score breakdown with night→morning correlation |
| Day 6 | Week preview + friend comparison (if applicable) |
| Day 7 | Full weekly summary + trial conversion |

Nothing hidden or locked — appears naturally as data accumulates. Prevents the RISE problem of "learned everything in 2 weeks."

### 5. The Night→Morning Feedback Loop

The feature no competitor has. Every morning shows EXACTLY how last night affected this morning:

- Day 2: "Last night: bed 22 min late. This morning: 67 (would've been 73)."
- Day 3: "Last night: bed 8 min late. This morning: 72. See what happens when the night works?"
- Day 4: "Last night: bed 41 min late. This morning: 68. You donated 41 min to your phone. Cost: 9 points."
- Day 5: "Pattern alert: your 3 best mornings followed your 3 earliest bedtimes. Coincidence? No. Biology."

By Day 5, the user has personally proven the core thesis with their own data.
