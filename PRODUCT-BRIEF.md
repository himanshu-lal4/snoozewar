## The Problem We're Solving

Most people are fighting their own biology every single morning — and they don't even know it.

**Social jet lag** is the gap between when your body naturally wants to sleep and wake up (your chronotype) and when society forces you to. It affects 69-70% of people worldwide — that's roughly 2.5 billion people waking up at the wrong time for their biology every single day.

The symptoms are universal:
- Setting 5-10 alarms and snoozing through all of them
- Scrolling your phone in bed for 30 minutes instead of getting up
- Saying "I'll go to bed early tonight" and still being awake at 2 AM
- Feeling lazy, guilty, and broken — wondering "what's wrong with me?"

**Here's what most people don't know:** Your chronotype (whether you're a natural early bird or night owl) is roughly 50% genetic. It's encoded in 351 gene variants. You can shift it by 1.8-2.4 hours max — but you can't will yourself into being a 5 AM person if your biology says otherwise.

People aren't lazy. They're chronically jet-lagged.

## Our Solution

An app that **understands your biology**, shows you why mornings have been so hard, and then **enforces** the schedule you actually need to keep — using neuroscience, not motivation.

### How It Works

**Step 1 — Discover (Onboarding, 2 minutes)**
You take a quick chronotype quiz. The app reveals:
- Your chronotype (what time your body naturally wants to sleep and wake)
- Your social jet lag — the gap between your biology and your real schedule ("You have a 2.1 hour mismatch")
- A reframe: "You're not lazy. You're not broken. 69% of people have this. It's biology, not willpower."

This is the aha moment. Years of self-blame reframed in 2 minutes. You finally understand WHY it's been so hard — and that understanding powers everything that comes next.

**Step 2 — Set Your Target**
The app asks: what time do you NEED to wake up? Not your biological ideal — your real-life requirement. Office at 9? Class at 8? Night shift at 11 PM? The app takes your required schedule and uses chronotype science to figure out the smartest way to get you there consistently.

If the gap between your biology and your schedule is small (under 2 hours), we can shift you closer. If it's bigger — like for night shift workers or extreme early schedules — we focus on building the habit as painlessly as possible, using what we know about your biology to time everything (bedtime, wind-down, light exposure) for maximum effect.

**Step 3 — Enforce (Every Night + Every Morning)**

*Night Mode:*
- Bedtime countdown calculated from your target wake time and chronotype
- Procrastination dares: "Still scrolling at 1 AM? Bold move. -5 points."
- The app challenges you to NOT procrastinate — reverse psychology that works

*Morning Mode:*
- Alarm with physical missions you can't fake (photo a specific spot, scan a barcode, shake your phone 50 times)
- Expiring morning content — a personalized digest that disappears if you don't get up in time (FOMO mechanic)
- One-tap morning journal: captures your mindset in 5 seconds

**Step 4 — Track (One Number)**

Your **Life Score** — a single number from 0 to 100 that lives on your home screen widget. It combines:
- How consistently you wake at your target time
- Whether you completed your morning actions
- Your streak length
- How well your bedtime supports your required schedule

It drops if you snooze. It drops if you skip. It drops if you go to bed late. One number that tells you if you're winning or losing today.

**Step 5 — Align (14-Day Program)**

"Shift Your Clock" — a guided 14-day program that gradually moves your sleep-wake cycle closer to your target schedule. 15 minutes every 2 days, using 5 scientifically proven time-shifters. Based on the Burgess protocol used in clinical chronotherapy. The program uses your chronotype data to determine the fastest, most sustainable path to your required schedule.

## The Science We Stand On

We're not making this up. Every major claim maps to published research:

- **Chronotype is genetic** — 351 gene variants identified across 697,828 people
- **Social jet lag is real** — named and measured by Till Roenneberg, one of the world's leading chronobiologists
- **69-70% affected** — not a marketing number, it's the actual prevalence in population studies
- **2+ hours of social jet lag doubles diabetes risk** — published longitudinal data
- **Sleep inertia disables your prefrontal cortex** — you literally cannot make good decisions for 15-70 minutes after waking
- **Habit formation takes 66 days on average** — the "21 days" number is a myth (Lally et al., UCL)
- **Loss aversion is 2-2.5x** — losing a 14-day streak hurts more than earning one, which is why our score mechanics work
- **Chronotype shifts relapse without maintenance** — the science behind why this is a subscription, not a one-time purchase

**What we'll never claim:**
- We don't promise to make you a morning person
- We don't say our app screen is "light therapy" (it's not — you need 2,500+ lux, a phone gives ~110)
- We don't use "willpower" framing (the willpower model has been debunked)
- We don't suggest melatonin in countries where it's prescription-only
