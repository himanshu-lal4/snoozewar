## KEY THEMES SUMMARY (for SnoozeWar competitive intelligence)

### What RISE Does Well (Defend Against)
1. Sleep debt as a single motivating metric — users love seeing the number go down
2. Energy schedule/circadian rhythm visualization — "when to do deep work"
3. Timed habit nudges (caffeine cutoff, wind-down, melatonin window)
4. Science-backed credibility — users cite this as differentiator
5. Apple Watch haptic alarm — genuinely beloved feature
6. Calendar sync with energy peaks
7. No upfront paywall before showing value

### Where RISE Fails (SnoozeWar Opportunities)
1. **Shift workers completely abandoned** — app is useless for non-standard schedules
2. **Trial-to-paid billing is rage-inducing** — "fraudulent bastards" level anger
3. **Energy predictions are one-size-fits-all** — medication, neurodivergence, conditions ignored
4. **Content gets repetitive fast** — "1-2 month bootcamp, not a forever tool"
5. **Battery drain** — 22-25% daily background usage reported
6. **No teen optimization** — parents want it for kids but it doesn't fit
7. **Orthosomnia risk** — tracking anxiety is real and unaddressed
8. **Price confusion** — multiple hidden tiers, sketchy auto-renewal
9. **Email unsubscribe broken** — dark pattern or incompetence
10. **Sleep data import errors** — historical data corrupts current calculations
11. **No enforcement** — "improving your sleep without enforcing anything" (Product Hunt quote) — RISE educates, doesn't enforce. SnoozeWar enforces.
12. **Graduated users** — once you learn your sleep debt and habits, no reason to keep paying

### SnoozeWar Positioning vs RISE
- RISE = "understand your sleep" (education/tracking)
- SnoozeWar = "own your morning" (enforcement/behavior change)
- RISE users graduate because insights plateau; SnoozeWar's streak/dare mechanics create ongoing engagement
- RISE ignores chronotype biology; SnoozeWar is built on it
- RISE has no social/competitive layer; SnoozeWar has accountability built in
