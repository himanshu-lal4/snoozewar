## Introduction: The "Vibe CEO" Mindset

You are no longer just a solo developer; you are the **Vibe CEO** of a virtual team. In 2026, the bottleneck is no longer raw coding hours—it is the quality of your **structured specifications** and your ability to **orchestrate AI agents**. 

Traditional startups burn 70-80% of their funding on salaries. By replacing headcount with a strategic AI tool stack, you can achieve 10-50x higher capital efficiency [1]. Your goal is to set the high-level direction and let specialized AI agents handle coding, marketing, design, and operations.

## 1. The Core Philosophy: From "Prompting" to "Context Engineering"

The single biggest differentiator between a good developer and a "best-in-the-world" solo founder is **Context Engineering**. You are not just writing prompts; you are architecting the entire information environment that makes your AI agents 10-50x more effective [1].

### The Context Stack
- **CLAUDE.md / AGENTS.md**: These are not just instruction files; they are the "Senior Engineer's Brain" for your codebase. They must encode:
    - **Architecture Decisions**: Why you chose Expo + React Native + Node.js + MongoDB.
    - **Coding Conventions**: Exact linting rules, naming patterns, and folder structures.
    - **Scientific Foundation**: The 50+ research papers and chronotype science that drive your app.
    - **Project State**: A living log of what was done, what's next, and why.
- **Structured Memory**: Treat your project context like a database. Store user personas, brand guidelines, and competitor analysis in structured Markdown/JSON files that agents can query.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**: Connect your AI agents directly to your database, analytics (PostHog), and deployment (Vercel/Expo) via MCP servers. This turns your AI from a "chat tool" into a "teammate" that can actually *see* and *act* on your infrastructure [1].

## 4. Advanced "Vibe" Workflows

- **Vibe Coding**: Describe the feature in natural language in your `CLAUDE.md` or a feature spec, and let the agent implement the entire vertical slice (DB schema -> API -> UI) [1].
- **Vibe Marketing**: Use a CrewAI team to analyze your `market/` research and generate a 30-day TikTok/Reddit content calendar, including scripts and ad copy, in minutes.
- **Vibe Ops**: Automate your entire "Day 0 to Day 30" user journey using Make.com or Gumloop, triggered by PostHog events.

## 5. Immediate Next Steps to Supercharge Your Project

1. **Upgrade your `CLAUDE.md`**: Move beyond simple instructions. Include your full "Science Foundation" and "Key Decisions" as non-negotiable constraints for every session.
2. **Implement MCP**: Set up MCP servers for your local environment so Claude can directly interact with your file system, terminal, and eventually your DB.
3. **Automate the "Pre-Dev Playbook"**: You have 130+ deliverables. Use a specialized **CrewAI** team to work through Phase 1 (Product Definition) and Phase 2 (UX & Design) in parallel while you focus on the core architecture.
4. **Shift to "Test-First"**: Before building any feature from your `mvp-features.md`, ask Claude to write the unit and integration tests based on the feature's PRD. This ensures "best-in-the-world" quality from day one.
