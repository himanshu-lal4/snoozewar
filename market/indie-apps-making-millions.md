# Indie/Small-Team Apps Making $1M-$20M+/Year on Subscriptions

> Research date: 2026-04-07
> Criteria: $1M-$20M+/year revenue, solo or <10 person teams, subscription-first, daily/frequent use, strong organic discovery
> Excluded categories: alarm/morning routine, habit tracking, meditation, budget/finance

---

## 1. Flighty (Flight Tracking)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Revenue** | ~$6M ARR ($500K/month) |
| **Team** | 3 people |
| **Category** | Travel / Utility |
| **Launched** | 2019 |
| **Platform** | iOS |
| **Pricing** | $5/week, $60/year, or $300 lifetime |

**Sources:** [Starter Story breakdown](https://www.starterstory.com/flighty-breakdown), [X post by Harsh Dwivedi](https://x.com/harsh_dwivedi7/status/1875453915178926574), [RevenueCat blog](https://www.revenuecat.com/blog/growth/ryan-jones-flighty-launched-podcast-2025/)

**Why it works:** Ryan Jones (ex-Apple) built this because airlines suck at real-time updates. The "digital passport" -- an auto-generated flight log -- is the #1 organic growth driver. People screenshot and share it. No paid ads. Growth comes from product quality and shareability. Frequent flyers check it multiple times per trip.

**Lesson:** Niche utility + beautiful design + organic shareability = $6M with 3 people.

## Patterns Worth Noting

### What the $1M-$10M indie apps have in common:
1. **Strong search intent** -- people Google/App Store search for exactly what these apps do
2. **Daily or near-daily use** -- weather, notes, workouts, plant watering, food scanning
3. **Organic virality baked into the product** -- Flighty's passport, Widgetsmith's home screen, CARROT's humor screenshots
4. **No content dependency** -- the app IS the value, no editorial team needed
5. **Solo or <10 person teams** -- GymStreak, Widgetsmith, CARROT Weather, Bear, Pixel Pals all under 5 people
6. **Niche-first, expand later** -- none of these tried to be everything at launch
7. **Platform features as growth levers** -- widgets (Widgetsmith), Dynamic Island (Pixel Pals), Apple Watch (CARROT)

### Revenue per employee (top performers):
- CARROT Weather: ~$2.4M per person
- Widgetsmith: ~$2.4M per person
- Pixel Pals: ~$2.5M per person
- GymStreak: ~$500K per person
- Flighty: ~$2M per person
- Opal: ~$909K per person

### Surprising niches that work:
- **Plant care** (Planta: $3.6M/year bootstrapped)
- **Food scanning** (Yuka: $7.3M/year, 98% subscriptions)
- **Flight tracking** (Flighty: $6M/year, 3 people)
- **Screen time/focus** (Opal: $10M/year, 11 people)
- **Weather with personality** (CARROT: $2.4M/year, 1 person)

### Categories where indie devs win on subscriptions:
1. Health & Fitness (highest revenue after 12 months per RevenueCat)
2. Utilities (73.6% of revenue from weekly subscriptions)
3. Photo & Video (fastest to $1K MRR)
4. Productivity (strong annual plan adoption)
