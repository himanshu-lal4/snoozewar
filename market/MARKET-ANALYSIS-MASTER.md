### Our Specific Market

**The "Behavior Enforcement App" category doesn't formally exist yet.** We're creating it.

The closest sizing: take the overlap of people who:
1. Use alarm apps (~200M active users globally)
2. AND want morning routine help (~120M habit app users)
3. AND will pay for enforcement (Alarmy + Opal prove willingness)

**Our direct addressable market: ~$800M - $1.5B**

This is derived from:
- Alarmy ($15-20M) + Sleep Cycle ($28M) + Opal ($10M) + Fabulous (<$1M) + Habitica ($5.3M) + long tail = ~$80-100M current revenue in our competitive set
- But these apps each capture only ONE piece of the cycle
- Full-cycle coverage (night → wake → morning → day) with gamification expands the ceiling 5-10x
- Similar to how Noom ($369M) created "behavior change weight loss" as a new premium category above MyFitnessPal ($120M)

**SOM:** Based on comparable launches — Opal hit $10M ARR in 2 years with 11 people (iOS only). Alarmy grew from $11M to $15M between 2021-2022. Our combination play + TikTok-native marketing should outpace both.

### Our Structural Advantage
**H&F has the BEST trial conversion (35%) but WORST renewal (30.3%).** Users sign up enthusiastically, then abandon.

Our app fixes this because:
1. **Alarm = forced daily usage** (can't ignore it)
2. **Streaks = loss aversion** (don't break the chain)
3. **Dare personality = emotional connection** (Duolingo proved this: 12% → 55% retention)
4. **Disappearing value = FOMO** (morning digest expires = reason to engage NOW)
5. **Life Score = status** (score goes down if you skip = OneScore obsession)
