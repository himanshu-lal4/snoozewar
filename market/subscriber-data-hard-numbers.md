## Key Takeaways For Our App

1. **H&F has the BEST trial-to-paid conversion (35%) but the WORST first-renewal retention (30.3%).** This means: easy to get people to pay, hard to keep them. Our "Dare Engine" personality and streaks need to solve the retention problem specifically.

2. **Hard paywall converts 5x better than freemium.** Our plan to use hard paywall + 7-day trial is validated by the data.

3. **Annual plans are king** -- 60-68% of H&F revenue, and Day-380 retention is 40% higher than monthly. Push annual aggressively.

4. **Even category leaders are DECLINING** -- Sleep Cycle (-16%), Calm (-12.5%), Headspace (-15%). The meditation/sleep market is saturating. Our "behavior enforcement" angle is the right differentiation.

5. **Opal proves lean + obsessed-with-conversion works.** $10M ARR with 11 people, 121 A/B tests on paywalls. We should plan for aggressive experimentation from day 1.

6. **Fabulous proves users != revenue.** 37M users, ~$1.2M ARR. Don't chase downloads -- chase conversion and retention.

7. **Calm's strategy of reducing free content from 90% to 5% tripled conversion (2% -> 7%).** Don't give away too much. Our hard paywall approach is correct.

8. **$14 LTV in India/SEA vs $32 in North America.** Price accordingly. Our INR 1,999/yr plan (~$24) is positioned well for India.
