## 7. Morning Routine App Specific Data

### Direct Competitors in "Morning Routine" Space
| App | Focus | Revenue Est. | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------------|-------|
| Fabulous | Morning routine + habits | ~$15M/yr | 37M users, Duke research-backed |
| Alarmy | Wake-up enforcement | ~$15-20M/yr | 82M downloads, behavior enforcement |
| Routinery | Time-based routines | <$1M | Niche, growing |
| Morning Routine | Simple routine timer | <$500K | Basic, low retention |

### Market Gap Analysis
- **No app owns the 5-30 minute post-wake "decision void"**
- Alarmy gets you OUT of bed but abandons you after
- Fabulous has routines but no enforcement mechanism
- No app combines: alarm enforcement + guided routine + productivity bridge
- Peak download times: 7-9 AM (15% of daily downloads) and 10 PM-1 AM (12%)
- This aligns perfectly with a sleep-to-wake-to-routine app's natural discovery moments

### User Behavior Signals
- Standalone habit tracking apps segment growing at 9.8% CAGR (2025-2033)
- North America: 35%+ of habit app revenue (high willingness to pay)
- Community-driven features increase retention 39% (Habitica data)
- AI-powered suggestions increase engagement 28% (Productive data)
